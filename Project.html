Trading is the speculative purchase and sale of financial instruments (stocks, bonds, futures contracts, options, currencies, etc.) with the intention of profiting off a change in price. An investor who buys financial instruments with the goal of selling them after an appreciation in price (over a year), the trader can make money when the instrument goes up or down in value, and does so over a considerably shorter period of time than an investor.
